DailyForge Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

DailyForge (“the App”) is developed and maintained by Michael Geehan. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

1. Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information, including your daily focus entry, is stored locally on your device and is never shared.

2. Notifications

If you choose to enable notifications, iOS handles all notification delivery. DailyForge does not transmit or store notification data outside your device.

3. Third-Party Services

DailyForge does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or third-party data services.

4. Children’s Privacy

DailyForge does not collect any personal information, and is safe for users of all ages.

5. Changes to This Policy

Updates may be made as new features are added. The most current version will always be available on this page.
